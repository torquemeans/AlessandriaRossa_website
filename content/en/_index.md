We are communists, specifically affiliated with the Communist Left. The purpose of the site is to collect in a light, accessible, and rational format the theoretical material inherent to the real movement that abolishes the present state of things. If anyone in our group ever goes to the trouble of engaging in revolutionary prose, this site is designed for that too. For questions, write to the email address located at the bottom of the page.
